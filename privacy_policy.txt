Habitually Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how our app collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Data Collection 
Our app collects data related to app performance, including crash logs, diagnostics, and other app performance information. This data is used to improve the functionality and performance of our app.

Data Use 
We use the data collected by our app to improve the functionality and performance of our app. We do not share any data collected by our app with third parties.

Data Storage 
We store the data collected by our app for as long as necessary to provide our services and improve the functionality and performance of our app.

Data Sharing 
We do not share any data collected by or provided to our app with third parties.

Data Security 
We take the security of your data seriously. Data is encrypted in transit to ensure that it is transferred over a secure connection.

User Rights 
You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. While we do not provide a way for you to request that your data be deleted, you can delete your data within the application itself.

Changes to this Privacy Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Information If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data collection practices, please contact us at support@habituallyimprove.app
